Automated Step Irrigation


Hello and Welcome everyone!

The Problem

The goal of this project is to prevent the death of plants due to lack of water or excess water that leads to waterlogging and eventually death.


The Solution

I have made this project for an interschool 3D printing competiiton, which was organised by Podar International School Nerul (ICSE) and we had participated in the competiiton. The project idea was solely thought out by me. I have used a water pump, analog moisture sensor and an Arduino Uno. I have not named this project anything though. I like to call it Automated Step Irrigation System.

Project Description and Working

The moisture sensor in this project senses the moisture of the soil and then sends it to the arduino uno. The uno then calculates the moisutre in percentage and compares it with 70%, if the moisture is more than 70%, the uno will not do anything while if it is less than 70%, the uno will start the water pump.
